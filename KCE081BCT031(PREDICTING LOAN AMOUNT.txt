KCE081BCT031(PREDICTING LOAN AMOUNTS)

Assigned EDA

1. Target Variable Analysis (LoanAmount)
● Analyze distribution of loan amounts
○ Mean, median, min, max, standard deviation

2. Demographic Analysis
● Analyze loan amount by:
○ Gender
○ Married
○ Dependents

● Identify patterns in loan allocation among different demographic groups
3. Education & Employment Analysis
● Examine Education and Self_Employed status vs loan amount
● Identify whether education or employment type influences approved loan amounts

4. Income Analysis
● Analyze ApplicantIncome and CoapplicantIncome vs LoanAmount
● Examine correlation between income and loan amount
● Identify thresholds for loan eligibility based on income

5. Loan Term & Interest Rate Analysis
● Analyze Loan_Amount_Term and Interest_Rate distribution
● Examine relationship between loan term, interest rate, and loan amount
● Identify optimal loan term ranges for larger loans

6. Credit History Analysis
● Analyze Credit_History vs loan amount
● Examine whether applicants with positive credit history receive higher loans
● Identify the impact of credit history on approved loan amounts

7. Property Area Analysis
● Analyze Property_Area vs loan amount
● Compare loan allocation across urban, semi-urban, and rural areas
● Identify patterns in property-based loan distribution

8. Loan Status Analysis
● Analyze Loan_Status vs loan amount
● Examine whether approved loans are skewed toward higher amounts
● Identify rejected applications and reasons if available

9. Interaction & Combined Effects
● Analyze combined effects of:
○ Income × Credit_History
○ Gender × LoanAmount
○ Education × ApplicantIncome
● Identify patterns affecting loan allocation

10. Feature Engineering Insights
● Engineer new features such as:
○ Total income (Applicant + Coapplicant)
○ Debt-to-income ratio
○ Loan-to-value proxy
○ Income bands or categories
